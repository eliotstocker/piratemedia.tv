## Company
A leader in self-serve researach and Insights tooling, Attest sets itself apart from competition by pairing state of the art technology with a team of caring and knowledgeable research and Insights professionals.
Attests key aim is to make research effortless and minimise the time from question to answer from days/weeks, down to minutes.

## Stack
The Attest platform primarily consists of a Spring boot 3 Java, Service oriented architecture. Postgres stores much of the data, and Kafka performs key asynchronous flows. Kubernetes lays the foundations of the stack and allows the agile team to continuously deliver the product.
